Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 4 large
Parmesan cheese 100g

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water.

Step 2: Fry pancetta until crispy.

Step 3: Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.

Step 4: Combine everything while pasta is hot.

Step 5: Serve with extra cheese and pepper.

Source: BBC Good Food

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2¼ cups
Butter 1 cup
Brown sugar ¾ cup
Chocolate chips 2 cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F.

Step 2: Mix butter and sugars until fluffy.

Step 3: Add eggs and vanilla, then dry ingredients.

Step 4: Fold in chocolate chips.

Step 5: Bake for 9-11 minutes until golden.

Source: Allrecipes
